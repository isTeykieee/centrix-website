Once I receive my coffee, how do I keep it fresh?

If you will drink a whole roast (a little over a pound) in two to three weeks, leave your coffee in the polyfoil degassing bag at room temperature. If you want to keep your coffee fresh longer than 3 weeks, freeze it in the original packaging. Freezing will slightly reduce the aroma of your brewed coffee, but will preserve the fresh flavor for up to three months. Whatever you do, please DO NOT refrigerate your coffee. Coffee is like baking soda. It will absorb any odors in your refrigerator resulting in a fresh-smelling 'fridge and strange-tasting coffee.
